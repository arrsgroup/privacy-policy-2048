2048 Ultra - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Introduction

2048 Ultra ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile game application ("App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • In-App Purchases: Transaction information (payment details processed securely by Google Play Store)
  • User Account Data: Username, email address, and preferences
  • Support Communications: Contact information and message content when you contact support

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game progress, scores, play time, app interaction data
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, performance metrics
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information about ads you view or interact with

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Game Functionality: Provide game features, save progress, enable multiplayer
  • Personalization: Customize gaming experience and store preferences
  • Analytics: Understand player behavior to improve gameplay
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
Privacy Commitment: We only collect data necessary to provide the best gaming experience. We never sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

AdMob (Google)

  • Purpose: Display advertisements within the App
  • Data Collected: Device information, advertising ID, app usage data
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Google Play Services

  • Purpose: Enable in-app purchases and game services
  • Data Collected: Purchase history, achievements, leaderboard data
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in structured format

Privacy Controls

  • Adjust device privacy settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements
  • Control app permissions (may affect functionality)

Children's Privacy

2048 Ultra is designed for general audiences and is suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. We do not require age verification and do not collect any personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.
